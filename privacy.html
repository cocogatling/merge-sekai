Merge Sekai - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Merge Sekai respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected while using the activity.

1. Information Collection

Merge Sekai currently does not intentionally collect, store, or sell personal information.

Some gameplay-related data may be temporarily stored locally on the user's device or browser for save functionality and gameplay features.

2. Discord Information

When launched through Discord Activities, Discord may provide limited account-related information through its platform APIs, such as usernames or user IDs, as required for activity functionality.

Merge Sekai does not sell or intentionally share this information with third parties.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Merge Sekai may use browser local storage or similar technologies to save gameplay progress and settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Merge Sekai operates through third-party platforms such as Discord and GitHub Pages. These services may collect technical or usage information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to maintain the security and stability of the activity, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Merge Sekai is not specifically directed toward children under the age required by Discord's Terms of Service.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated or modified at any time.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the developer through Discord.
